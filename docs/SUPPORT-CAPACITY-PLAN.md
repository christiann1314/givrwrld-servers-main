## Capacity and limits

- **Current capacity:** How many tickets/Discord threads can you **reliably** answer per day (or per week) without dropping threads or delaying too long?  
  Example: “We can handle ~X tickets per day and ~Y Discord threads; beyond that we need to stagger or add help.”

- **Campaign rule:** Before a big launch (ads, influencer push, big Reddit post, etc.):  
  - Decide if you’re **capping** signups or traffic (e.g. waitlist, limited slots), or  
  - **Stagger** the campaign (e.g. roll out in phases) so support volume stays within capacity.

- **If you’re over capacity:** Post a short notice (e.g. in Discord and on status/support): “We’re seeing higher-than-usual volume; responses may take longer. We’ll get to everyone in order.” Then prioritize: payment/server issues first, then setup questions, then general pre-sales.
