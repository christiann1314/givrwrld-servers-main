# Comparison & affiliate pitch kit

One-pager for reviewers, listicles, and affiliates. Use for consistent messaging and “what we need from you.”

---

## Pricing

- **By game:** See [Deploy](https://givrwrld.com/deploy) and the configurator for each game (Minecraft, Rust, Palworld, Ark, Terraria, Factorio, etc.). Plans start at different price points by RAM (e.g. 2GB, 4GB, 8GB).
- **By term:** Monthly, quarterly, and yearly. Longer terms have lower effective monthly price. All prices are shown before checkout.
- **No hidden fees.** You see the full price before payment. PayPal subscriptions; manage or cancel from your dashboard.

*Update this section with a short “starting from $X” by game if you lock in canonical numbers for a campaign.*

## What we need from you (reviewers / affiliates)

- **Accuracy:** Use the same numbers we publish (typical time to ready from Status, same DDoS and guarantee lines). Don’t overclaim.
- **Links:** Link to [givrwrld.com](https://givrwrld.com), [Deploy](https://givrwrld.com/deploy), or game-specific pages (e.g. [Minecraft](https://givrwrld.com/deploy/minecraft)). Affiliate links when the program is live.
- **Disclosure:** Disclose affiliate or sponsored relationships where required.
- **Questions:** For pricing, migration, or “what plan for X?” use our [FAQ](https://givrwrld.com/faq) and [Support](https://givrwrld.com/support), or [Discord](https://givrwrld.com/discord).
